Who We Are

Office Max is an experienced market supplier of office products and services. Sales are processed through multiple channels, consisting of office supply stores, a contract sales force, Internet sites, direct marketing catalogs, and call centers, all supported by its network of cross docks, warehouses, and delivery operations.

The Company is organized into three core business segments:

  • Office Stationery
  • Professional Printing
  • Housekeeping

Today, Office Max is one of the largest sellers of office products and an industry leader in every distribution channel, including stores, direct mail, contract delivery, and business-to-business electronic commerce.

Our Journey & Values

We started our journey in the year 2020 with the name of Office Max. With esteemed hard work and dedication since 2005 under a different entity, we incorporated into Office Max. The firm, which started off with a small office but with dreams of succeeding in this competitive market in India, has ever since its inception laid down its own values. The basic guiding principles of honesty, integrity, and commitment have metamorphosed it into a PAN India company in the field of stationery, printing, analog & digital procurement solutions.


In the competitive outsourcing industry, it's difficult to know what differentiates one company from the next. With Office Max, you can be certain that we put our clients first—we take a unique approach to fostering client satisfaction.

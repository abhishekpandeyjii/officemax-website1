Who We Are

Office Max is an experienced market supplier of office products and services. Sales are processed through multiple channels, consisting of office supply stores, a contract sales force, Internet sites, direct marketing catalogs, and call centers, all supported by its network of cross docks, warehouses, and delivery operations.

Today, Office Max is one of the largest sellers of office products and an industry leader in every distribution channel, including stores, direct mail, contract delivery, and business-to-business electronic commerce.
